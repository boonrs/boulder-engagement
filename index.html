How do we rate civic engagement?

  • Broad Reach: reaching diverse groups within the community (particularly those who are typically under-represented)
    • residents, students, workers, community organizations
    • representation across ethnicities, neighborhoods, resident tenure, income levels, educational levels, digital literacy levels
  • Appropropriate and Relevant Information: (providing relevant info (using language that resonates with the individual)
    • information that is relevant, unbiased, easy to understand and organized ‘from the user’s point of view’
    • information that is easy to access and offered in preferred languages
  • Diversity of Forums: providing easy and accessible forums for voices and hands (meeting people where they are)
    • providing opportunities for face-to-face interaction tailored to people’s location and schedule
    • use of digital tools to facilitate interactive dialogue
  • Use of Feedback Loops: incorporating feedback and demonstrating how it influenced outcomes
    • using input to inform decisions and approaches
    • communicating how decisions and approaches were influenced by input
